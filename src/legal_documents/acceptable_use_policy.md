## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### 1.1 Policy Objective

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") establishes the standards of conduct for all users of the Legal AI System ("Service," "Platform"). This Policy is designed to:
- Ensure responsible and ethical use of the Service
- Protect the integrity of the legal profession
- Maintain compliance with professional conduct rules
- Safeguard the security and reliability of the Platform
- Prevent illegal or harmful activities

### 1.2 Scope of Application

This Policy applies to:
- All registered users of the Legal AI System
- Licensed attorneys and legal professionals
- Paralegals and legal support staff
- Pro se litigants and self-represented individuals
- Client users with limited access
- All activities conducted through the Platform

### 1.3 Binding Nature

By accessing or using the Legal AI System, you agree to comply with this Acceptable Use Policy. Violation of this Policy may result in:
- Immediate suspension or termination of account access
- Referral to appropriate professional licensing authorities
- Legal action for damages or injunctive relief
- Cooperation with law enforcement investigations

## 2. PROHIBITED USES

### 2.1 Illegal Activities

**Strictly Prohibited:**
- Using the Service for any illegal purpose or activity
- Facilitating or encouraging criminal conduct
- Money laundering or financial crimes
- Tax evasion or fraud schemes
- Immigration fraud or document falsification
- Securities fraud or investment schemes
- Identity theft or impersonation
- Cybercrime or computer fraud

### 2.2 Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL)

**Non-Attorney Users:**
- May not provide legal advice to third parties using Platform information
- May not represent others in legal proceedings using Platform tools
- May not hold themselves out as attorneys or legal experts
- May not establish attorney-client relationships through the Platform
- Must clearly disclaim any legal expertise when sharing Platform information

**Out-of-Jurisdiction Practice:**
- Attorneys may not use the Platform to practice law in jurisdictions where not licensed
- Must comply with multi-jurisdictional practice rules
- May not provide legal advice on matters governed by unfamiliar law
- Must refer clients to appropriately licensed counsel when necessary

### 2.3 Professional Misconduct

**Ethical Violations:**
- Client confidentiality breaches
- Conflicts of interest violations
- Fee-splitting arrangements prohibited by ethics rules
- Solicitation of clients in violation of advertising rules
- Frivolous or harassing litigation tactics
- Misrepresentation of qualifications or experience
- Failure to maintain professional competence

### 2.4 Platform Abuse

**Technical Violations:**
- Attempting to gain unauthorized access to the System
- Reverse engineering, decompiling, or hacking the Platform
- Introducing malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Overloading or interfering with System operation
- Circumventing security measures or access controls
- Creating multiple accounts to evade restrictions
- Sharing account credentials with unauthorized persons

**Content Abuse:**
- Uploading inappropriate, offensive, or irrelevant content
- Submitting false, misleading, or inaccurate information
- Violating intellectual property rights of others
- Sharing confidential information without authorization
- Using the Platform for personal, non-legal activities
- Spamming or sending unsolicited communications

### 2.5 Harassment and Discrimination

**Prohibited Conduct:**
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of other users
- Discriminatory conduct based on protected characteristics
- Threats of violence or harm
- Hate speech or offensive communications
- Stalking or persistent unwanted contact
- Creating a hostile environment for other users

## 3. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Attorney Professional Responsibilities

**Competence and Diligence:**
- Maintain competence in areas of practice
- Provide diligent representation to clients
- Stay current with legal and technological developments
- Understand limitations of AI-generated content
- Seek additional training when necessary

**Client Relations:**
- Maintain clear boundaries regarding Platform use
- Explain AI limitations to clients
- Obtain informed consent for AI-assisted work
- Protect client confidentiality at all times
- Avoid conflicts of interest

**Professional Independence:**
- Exercise independent professional judgment
- Not rely solely on AI-generated advice or analysis
- Review and verify all AI-generated content
- Take responsibility for all work product
- Maintain professional accountability

### 3.2 Paralegal and Support Staff Requirements

**Supervision Requirements:**
- Work under appropriate attorney supervision
- Do not provide legal advice independently
- Refer all legal questions to supervising attorney
- Maintain confidentiality of client information
- Follow all professional conduct guidelines

**Competence and Training:**
- Maintain competence in assigned tasks
- Seek training on Platform features and limitations
- Report technical issues or concerns promptly
- Follow established protocols and procedures
- Document work appropriately

### 3.3 Pro Se User Requirements

**Self-Representation Acknowledgments:**
- Understand risks of self-representation
- Acknowledge limitations of AI assistance
- Do not provide legal advice to others
- Comply with all court rules and procedures
- Seek professional counsel when appropriate

**Information Use Limitations:**
- Use Platform information for personal legal matters only
- Do not share or distribute Platform-generated content commercially
- Verify all information with appropriate authorities
- Understand jurisdiction-specific requirements
- Take responsibility for all legal decisions
